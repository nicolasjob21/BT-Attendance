Objectives

What the system must accomplish, and the standard it is held to because it feeds payroll directly.

Trust the data

Verified attendance

Every clock-in is proven by a selfie, GPS coordinates validated against a site geofence, and a server-side timestamp — so no log entering payroll is disputable.

Remove the manual work

Automated payroll

Worked hours, overtime premiums, holiday pay, late/undertime, and statutory deductions compute automatically per cutoff — no spreadsheets.

Stay compliant

Correct by law

2026 SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG rates plus DOLE leave rules are built in and configurable, and personal data is handled under RA 10173.

Fit both teams

Office & field

Production-ready

Queued bulk payslip generation, HTTPS-everywhere, and role-based access for Admin, HR, and Technical users.

02

Scope & staff model

Two employee types drive different attendance and overtime rules — the system treats them distinctly from clock-in through pay computation.

Admin staff · web / app

Fixed 8:30–5:30 shift

  • Clocks in at HQ against a fixed schedule
  • Late and undertime auto-flagged against the shift
  • Straightforward day-count and hours computation
Technical staff · mobile

Flexible shift, site trips

  • Checks in to client sites, not just HQ — captures site, client, and travel time
  • Trip-based, frequent overtime with supervisor sign-off
  • Offline clock-in that syncs when signal returns
  • Minimum-hours rule so OT computes against a baseline
In scope: attendance capture, scheduling, OT & trip approval, leave management, payroll computation, payslips, and reporting. Out of scope (phase 1): biometric hardware integration, accounting-system sync, and mobile app store packaging (the mobile flow runs as a responsive web app first).

04

Functional modules

Six modules, built in dependency order so each rests on clean data from the one before it.

Module 01

Attendance

Web/mobile clock-in/out with selfie, GPS, and timestamp matched against the assigned location or a registered client-site radius.

  • Auto-flag late / undertime for fixed shifts
  • Field/trip mode captures site, client & travel time
  • Offline capture with later sync
Module 02

Overtime & field trips

Request → supervisor approval workflow for the frequent, trip-based OT of technical staff.

  • Regular vs rest-day vs holiday OT (different multipliers)
  • Travel time / per-diem tracking for trips outside Manila
Module 03

Payroll

Semi-monthly engine: basic pay, OT premiums, night differential, holiday pay, and absence/late deductions.

  • Auto-deduction of SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG & withholding tax
  • Queued bulk runs per cutoff — no request timeouts
Module 04

Government contributions

SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG computed from a configurable rate table — not hardcoded, because brackets change almost yearly.

  • Bracket lookup by salary and effective year
  • Employee & employer share both tracked
Module 05

Leave management

PH-standard leave types with a request/approval workflow tied into the attendance calendar.

  • SIL, VL, SL, maternity (105d), paternity (7d), solo parent
  • Approved leave auto-excludes days from absences
Module 06

Reports & analytics

Exports and dashboards for HR, finance, and management.

  • DTR export & payroll register
  • Government remittance reports
  • OT / trip cost analysis per technical staff

The computation rules the payroll engine applies. All rates are stored in a configurable table with an effective year, so the 2026 figures below become a data update — not a code change — when they revise.

06

Data privacy & risk management

Capturing selfies, location, and timestamps for employees is regulated activity. These are the risks we plan for up front — not after go-live.

RA 10173 · Data Privacy Act

Personal data handling

Selfie + location + timestamp is sensitive personal data. We build in employee consent forms, a defined retention policy, and access limited to HR/Admin roles — auditable per the Data Privacy Act.

GPS drift

Geofence accuracy

GPS is unreliable indoors and near high-rises. We set a practical radius (100–150m) with a supervisor-approved manual override for legitimate edge cases at client sites.

Connectivity

Offline capability

Technical staff on out-of-town trips often have poor signal. Offline clock-in stored locally and synced later prevents gaps that would otherwise break OT logging.

Infrastructure note: the browser camera (getUserMedia) and geolocation APIs only work over HTTPS. Staging and production need a valid SSL certificate from day one — this is a hard requirement, not a nice-to-have.
